## General Questions - FAQ


#### How is the payout calculated?

The 5% treasury fee is taken from the total pool first, and the remaining reward pool is shared among everyone on the winning side, in proportion to their bet:

- Reward Pool: Total Pool × (1 − Treasury Fee)
- Payout Ratio for HIGHER: Reward Pool ÷ Amount Bet on HIGHER
- Payout Ratio for LOWER: Reward Pool ÷ Amount Bet on LOWER
- Your Payout: Your Bet × Payout Ratio (this includes your original stake back)

##### Suppose you bet 8 BONE on the LOWER position.
- Total LOWER pool: 60 BONE
- Total HIGHER pool: 90 BONE
- Total pool: 150 BONE
- Treasury fee: 5%

##### Step 1: Apply the Treasury Fee (5%)
Reward Pool = Total Pool × (1 – 0.05) = 150 × 0.95 = 142.5 BONE

##### Step 2: Calculate the Payout Ratio
Payout Ratio = Reward Pool ÷ LOWER Pool = 142.5 ÷ 60 = 2.375x

##### Step 3: Calculate Your Payout
Payout = Your Bet × Payout Ratio = 8 × 2.375 = 19 BONE

##### Step 4: Calculate Profit
Profit = Payout – Your Bet = 19 – 8 = 11 BONE

#### What are the fees?
5% of each round's total pot goes to the treasury 🛠️. The fee supports platform development, resources, and upkeep, and a portion is contributed to S.O.U. to support victims of the bridge hack 🤝. No fee is taken on refunded rounds.

#### Where does the price come from?
The SHIB/USD price is delivered by **Chainlink Data Streams**, a decentralized oracle network. Every price used to lock or end a round arrives as a cryptographically signed report that is verified on-chain before it can be used. There is no manual price entry or override — if a valid signed report isn't available, the round simply cannot be settled and bets become refundable. See [How It Works Under the Hood](technical-overview) for details.

#### Is there a time limit before I can collect my winnings?
No, collect your winnings 💰 at any time.

#### What happens if the round ends in a tie?
If the close price is exactly equal to the locked price, neither side wins — the round goes to the house and the full pot is sent to the treasury. Ties are rare, but they are a defined outcome of the game.

#### What if everyone bets on the same side?
If a round only has predictions on one side:

- If that side turns out to be **correct**, everyone gets their full stake back — no fee is taken, because there is no opposing pool to win from.
- If that side is **wrong** (or the round ties), the pot goes to the treasury.

#### What happens if a round fails to settle?
Each round must be settled within a short buffer window after it ends. If that doesn't happen — for example, because the price feed was interrupted or the game was paused — the round is voided and every participant can reclaim their full staked amount through the normal Collect flow. Refunds are always for 100% of your bet.

#### Can I change or remove my position?
No. Once you confirm a prediction, it is locked in for that round. You can also only enter once per round per wallet.

#### Is there a minimum bet?
Yes, a small minimum bet amount applies to every prediction. The exact amount is shown in the app when you enter a round.

#### Why can't I play from my multisig or smart wallet?
For safety, the Prediction contract only accepts bets from regular wallet addresses (EOAs). Smart-contract wallets — including multisigs and some account-abstraction wallets — cannot enter rounds.

#### The round's result changed after the round ended! Why?
Due to blockchain confirmation and data processing, final results may briefly update after the “live” round finishes.
