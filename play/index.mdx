## How It Works:

- Predict whether the price of SHIB/USD will go HIGHER or LOWER in a 5-minute round.
- Stake your BONE tokens on your prediction.
- If your prediction is correct when the round ends, you win a share of the prize pool!
- Collect your winnings 💰 at any time—you are never rushed.

## Ready to Start :

- Connect your wallet that holds BONE tokens.
- Check the countdown timer ⏱️ for the current round — make sure there's at least 30 seconds left before making your prediction.
- Choose Enter HIGHER to predict the price will rise, or Enter LOWER to predict it will fall.
- Enter the amount of BONE you want to commit and confirm the transaction.
- Wait for the round to complete automatically.
- If you win, claim your winnings easily with the Collect Winnings button 🏆.

## Important Notes :

- Once you enter a prediction, it is locked in and cannot be changed or removed. You can enter only one prediction per round, and a small minimum bet applies.
- The SHIB/USD price is sourced from **Chainlink Data Streams** and cryptographically verified on-chain before any round is locked or settled — nobody can manually set or override the price. Learn more in [How It Works Under the Hood](technical-overview).
- A 5% treasury fee is applied to each round's total pot 🛠️. The fee supports platform development and upkeep, and a portion is contributed to S.O.U. to support victims of the bridge hack 🤝.
- If the price at the end of a round is exactly the same as the locked price (a tie), the round goes to the house.
- Prize pools and multipliers dynamically adjust based on all users' predictions.
- If a round ever can't be settled in time (for example, the price feed is interrupted), your staked BONE becomes refundable in full — collect it with the same Collect Winnings flow.
- Due to blockchain finalization, results may sometimes update a few seconds after the round ends.
